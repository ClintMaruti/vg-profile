What We Have Achieved As A Ministry

The Lord has been gracious to us and last year we were able to have several meetings and we saw the power of God working through healing, deliverance, saving of souls, unlocking destinies and restoring lives.

In the month of August 10th 2018, we launched our first event on worship, called ONE NIGHT WITH THE KING WORSHIP EXPERIENCE ELDORET EDITION and from that, 10 souls gave their lives to Christ.

The same month of August the last weekend on Saturday we launched the widows and single mothers prayer breakfast in Eldoret started with 10 widows and 6 single mothers. We had our guest from Germany who ministered to them and blessed each one of them with 2 packets of flour 5 of them gave their lives to Christ.

And the Lord has been faithful and gracious and he has always made provision and touching the heart of people to give towards the ministry of widows and single mothers.

The month of December 21st 2018 we had our second edition of ONE NIGHT WITH THE KING WORSHIP EXPERIENCE KISUMU EDITION. 6 people gave their lives to Chrust.

The same month we had a colourful wedding from one of our team members was getting married and I was the event planner it was such a beautiful event.

This year we celebrated one year of God's goodness and in this year the ministry has expanded in number we have now 35 widows and single mothers we are helping in Eldoret, Bungoma, Tulumba - 12 and Kisumu - 7.

This year 2019 the month of February 1st 2019 we launched again ONE NIGHT WITH A KING WORSHIP EXPERIENCE, TANZANIA MWANZA EDITION and the presence of God was evident; lives were transformed, healing took place, the Holy Spirit took over the meeting and we saw God touching lives, 25 souls gave their lives to jesus Christ and we giveall the glory to Jesus.

We are supporting 5 orphans by paying their school fees and upkeep.
